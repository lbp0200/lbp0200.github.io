HostUS洛杉矶节点有三条线路

  • LAX01: Cogent, PCCW, GTT / Tinet and peering
  • LAX02: 联通、电信CN2，只有KVM套餐，价格较贵 Comcast, Hurricane Electric, PCCW, GTT / Tinet, Telia, China Telecom / CN2, China Unicom and peering
